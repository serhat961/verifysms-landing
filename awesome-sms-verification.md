## Paid Services

### Mobile Apps

- **[VerifySMS](https://verifysms.app)** — 150+ countries, non-VoIP carrier numbers, 15 languages, automatic refunds, iOS app. Starting at $0.10/number. Works with WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Google, Instagram, TikTok and 500+ platforms. Pay-as-you-go, no subscription.

### Web-Based Services

- **[5sim.net](https://5sim.net)** — Russian-based service, ~180 countries, developer API, lower prices for bulk.
- **[SMSPool](https://smspool.net)** — US-based, 50+ countries, 300+ services, crypto payments accepted. Mobile app available.
- **[SMS-Man](https://sms-man.com)** — Turkey-based, API access, competitive pricing, strong for automation use cases.
- **[TextVerified](https://textverified.com)** — US-focused, premium pricing, reliable for US numbers, strong reputation in dev community.
- **[GrizzlySMS](https://grizzlysms.com)** — Newer entrant, broad country coverage, API available.
- **[OnlineSim](https://onlinesim.io)** — Russian-based, ~200 countries, legacy service with long track record.
- **[SMSActivate](https://sms-activate.io)** — Historical leader in the niche (status changed late 2025). Large user base was migrated to alternatives.

### Burner Apps (Full Phone Replacement)

- **[Hushed](https://hushed.com)** — US/Canada focused, full phone replacement with calls + texts. Monthly subscription model.
- **[Burner](https://burnerapp.com)** — Mobile-first burner number app, US/Canada, calls + texts + voicemail.
- **[MySudo](https://mysudo.com)** — Multi-persona privacy app with phone, email, and browsing under one umbrella.
- **[Google Voice](https://voice.google.com)** — Free US-only service, VoIP based (does not work for WhatsApp/Google verification).
- **[TextNow](https://textnow.com)** — Free ad-supported VoIP US numbers.

## Free Services (Use with Caution)

**Important:** Free SMS sites use shared numbers that are often already burned on major platforms. Security risks include public message visibility, unreliable delivery, and lack of privacy protection.

- **[Receive-SMS](https://receive-smss.com)** — Free shared numbers, messages publicly visible.
- **[SMS24](https://sms24.me)** — Free disposable numbers.
- **[TempSms](https://temp-sms.org)** — Temporary numbers for basic verification.
- **[FreePhoneNum](https://freephonenum.com)** — Free US and Canadian numbers.
- **[QuackrIO](https://quackr.io)** — Limited free tier plus paid plans.

See also: [VerifySMS vs Free SMS Sites](https://verifysms.app/blog/verifysms-vs-free-sms-sites/)

## Related Tools

### Email Aliases (Privacy Companion Tools)
- **[SimpleLogin](https://simplelogin.io)** — Open source email alias service.
- **[addy.io](https://addy.io)** — Anonymous email forwarding with GPG support.
- **[Firefox Relay](https://relay.firefox.com)** — Mozilla-operated email alias service.
- **[Apple Hide My Email](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT210425)** — Built-in iCloud+ email alias feature.

### Virtual Credit Cards
- **[Privacy.com](https://privacy.com)** — Virtual one-time-use debit cards (US only).
- **[Revolut](https://revolut.com)** — Virtual cards for online purchases.

### Password Managers (for Privacy Hygiene)
- **[Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com)** — Open source, free tier.
- **[1Password](https://1password.com)** — Premium password manager with phone-alias integration.
- **[Proton Pass](https://proton.me/pass)** — From Proton, includes email aliases.

### Secure Messengers (Verified via Virtual Number)
- **[Signal](https://signal.org)** — End-to-end encrypted, open source.
- **[Session](https://getsession.org)** — No phone number required at signup.
- **[SimpleX Chat](https://simplex.chat)** — Fully anonymous messaging.
